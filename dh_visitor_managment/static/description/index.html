Description

The Visitor Management Module provides a streamlined and efficient way to manage and track visitors within an organization. It allows businesses to monitor visitor interactions, record essential details, and ensure a structured check-in/out process. With this system, users can easily capture visitor information, track visit purposes, and maintain detailed logs of interactions. Additionally, it offers a structured way to associate visitors with employees and departments while generating visitor passes for seamless identification.

Key Highlights

  • Visitor Tracking: Maintain a complete log of visitor details, including contact information and check-in/out records.
  • Meeting Management: Track which employees visitors are meeting and the purpose of their visit.
  • Visitor Pass Generation: Print detailed visitor passes with all relevant information.
  • QR Code Validation: Validate visitor entries using QR codes for quick verification.
